OpenCart - Data Sweeper

Minimum version tested: 1.5.6.x
Maximum version tested: 3.0.x.x

---------------------------------
Installation instructions
---------------------------------

1. Upload the file to the root of your OpenCart installation.
2. Visit the URL yoursite.com/opencartdatasweeper579.php
3. Read the instructions and when you're ready check the box and click delete data.
4. Your OpenCart installation is now clean.
5. Delete the opencartdatasweeper579.php from your root folder.
